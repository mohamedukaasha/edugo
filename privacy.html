Privacy Policy

How we protect your data across the Edugo ecosystem.

Introduction

At Edugo, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform or use our services.

Our primary goal is to provide a minimalist, highly efficient learning environment while ensuring your digital rights are never compromised.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, enroll in a course, or contact our support team. This may include:

  • Name and contact information for terminal identification.
  • Payment and billing details via secure, encrypted channels.
  • Learning progress and course completions for certification.
  • Direct communication history with industry mentors.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Personalizing your learning experience based on your trajectory.
  • Processing transactions and providing immediate access to paths.
  • Facilitating scheduled mentorship sessions and video calls.
  • Sending critical updates regarding platform security and curriculum.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Our stack uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS for all data in transit between our clusters and your terminal.
